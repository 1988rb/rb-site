About R.B. Exports

Based in Mysuru, India, R.B. Exports is a merchant export company specializing in the sourcing and supply of industrial products and consumables across diverse sectors. We act as a reliable link between manufacturers and end-users, ensuring consistent quality, competitive sourcing, and dependable delivery.

With over 30 years of long-standing association with the Group, we bring deep operational understanding, industry familiarity, and supply-chain insight to every engagement. Our approach is built on clarity, accountability, and long-term collaboration.
